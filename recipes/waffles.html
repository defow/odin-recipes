Classic Waffles

Description

A delicious and easy to make waffles recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 and a half cups warm milk
  • A third of cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
